Overview

MLR Institute of Technology, on its remarkable 16-year journey, has emerged as a hub for academic excellence, consistently achieving a placement rate of 80% and above each year. Our institution takes pride in guiding students toward success, fostering a culture of continuous learning and personal growth. We are dedicated to identifying and nurturing the unique virtues of each student, playing a pivotal role in expanding their intellectual horizons and contributing to their holistic personality development. At MLR, we believe in the proximal development of every student, ensuring a transformative experience throughout their academic journey. Our commitment is evident in the seamless progression from an aspiring engineering student to a self-motivated expert with versatile skills, ready to tackle challenges in various domains. This metamorphosis is facilitated by the Training & Placements wing at MLR Institute of Technology.
